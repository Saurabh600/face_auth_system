Why Face Authentication?

Biometric login eliminates every weakness of traditional passwords

Unbreakable Security

Unlike passwords, your face cannot be guessed, stolen from a database, or phished by fake login pages.

Instant Access

Face recognition completes in under 500 milliseconds. No typing, no remembering complex passwords.

Liveness Detection

The system works with your live face, not a photo. Spoof attempts with printed images are rejected.

Audit Trail

Every login attempt (success or failure) is logged with timestamp and IP address for security analysis.

AI-Powered Matching

128-dimensional face encodings from dlib's deep neural network achieve 99.38% accuracy on LFW benchmark.

Intruder Detection

Unknown faces are detected and denied instantly. Every unauthorized attempt is logged for review.

How It Works

Three simple steps to secure biometric authentication

01
Register

Create your account and allow the webcam to capture 30 face images to build your biometric profile.

02
Scan

On the login page, allow webcam access. The AI captures your face and computes a 128-dimensional encoding.

03
Access

Your face encoding is matched against stored profiles. Verified users gain instant access. Unknown faces are denied.
